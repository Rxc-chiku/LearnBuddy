Our Story

The journey behind LearnBuddy's mission to transform education

LearnBuddy was born from a simple belief: everyone deserves access to quality computer science education, regardless of their background or circumstances.

Founded by passionate educators and AI enthusiasts, we recognized the gap between traditional learning methods and the rapid evolution of technology. Our mission became clear - create an intelligent, adaptive learning companion that makes CS concepts accessible, engaging, and practical.

LearnBuddy is committed to breaking down barriers in tech education by providing accessible, adaptive learning experiences that cater to individual needs, learning styles, and pace. We strive to empower learners with practical skills that translate directly to real-world applications.
